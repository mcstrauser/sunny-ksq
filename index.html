No municipal expansion without a strategic plan.

A ~$10M police facility is heading toward construction bids and borrowing — and the first of several possible tax increases is already in effect, with more expected. Before any irreversible step, we're asking the Board of Supervisors to adopt and publish a clear, public, data-driven strategic plan.

What we stand for

The demand, plainly stated

Pause the police building project until Kennett Township adopts a public strategic plan and right-sizing resolution. We're not against meeting facility needs — we're against final approval before residents can see the need, cost, alternatives, and long-term impact.
